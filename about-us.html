Short History & Roadmap

When old ideas take new shapes.

  1. Genesis

    2014 - 2018

    The building blocks of Thaleria date back to 2014 when we tried to imagine what an ideal version of IT consulting could look like. We launched a first iteration through a partnership in the European Institutions market, which although very successful was also far from perfect. The founding principles were sound, but the growth of the company outpaced its processes. The team gradually improved the model over 4 years, but it still felt like an upgrade was needed – a v2.0 was a natural evolution.

  2. Development

    October-December 2018

    In October 2018 the team started working on Thaleria as an extension of the model to the private market. This was the perfect opportunity to go back to the drawing board and start with a clean slate. The last quarter of 2018 was spent reviewing the founding principles, building a new infrastructure and troubleshooting processes and best practices. After ironing out the kinks and quirks, Thaleria officially launched its operations.

  3. Thaleria IT Services

    January 2019

    With a working, competitive and reliable model, Thaleria launched full-scale activities within the IT Services market in January 2019. We hope our battle-tested principles and processes are a positive force in the development of both our clients and consultants – all the while keeping a solemn commitment to continuous improvement by listening to the market’s feedback.

  4. Thaleria Digital Services

    April 2019

    As IT Services grows, Thaleria is already preparing an offering for the Digital Marketing sector. As the world becomes increasingly connected, Thaleria recognizes both the importance and the challenges that many of our partners face in adapting and finding the right resources in this field. The launch of this business line is scheduled for April 2019.

  5. Thaleria Management Services

    July 2019

    Thaleria being born of a very specific expertise, it only makes sense to share that expertise with our partners and clients too. Thaleria intends on empowering its partners by sharing its best practices and its competitive edge. Our offering will start with our core expertise, in the form of building comprehensive HR strategies to help our partners face the challenges of talent-driven markets.

  6. Thaleria Labs

    January 2020

    Thaleria Labs will be the branch of the company dedicated to R&D, start-up incubation and rapid iteration of concepts and ideas. Naturally a certain scale is required for resources to be available for this, but Thaleria is already accompanying at least one start-up in its development (Paintify), and already is already pursuing business development efforts in the AI and Blockchain sectors ahead of its launch in 2020.

  7. Thaleria Foundation

    January 2021

    Thaleria has always believed that the business community has an enormous responsibility in shaping the society and world we live in. From the first day Thaleria was founded, 20% of every euro we make is allocated to a Social Responsibility Fund which will be used for social impact projects. While the Fund grows through 2019-2020, it will be picked up for use in January 2021 by the Thaleria Foundation.
